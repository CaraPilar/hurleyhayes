Getting to Chattanooga

  • Chattanooga Metropolitan Airport (CHA): 20 minute drive
  • McGhee Tyson Airport (TYS) (Knoxville): 1 hr 45 min drive
  • Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL): 2 hr drive
  • Huntsville International Airport (HSV): 2 hr drive
  • Consider Groome Airport Shuttle Service if traveling from the Atlanta area.

Transportation in Chattanooga

  • Free Downtown Shuttle
  • Bikeshare
  • Parking in Downtown Chattanooga

Travel Times

  • Tennessee Aquarium: 10 minute walk
